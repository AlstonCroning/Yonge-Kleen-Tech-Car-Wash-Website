While the car is being serviced, what are things I can do in the area?

There are plenty of things you can do.

Tim Hortons is within walking distance

Cafe is located on the main floor

Many restaurants are located to the North and South of Yonge street

Grocery stores such as Metro are within walking distance

What are the best ways to contact us?

If you want to contact us during our working hours, then the best way to reach us is by phone: (416) 223-4253 ext. 26

Outside of work hours, you may contact us using our cellphone (647) 708-8594 or email us at yongekleentech@gmail.com. We do not recommend calling the cellphone number at work due to the location of the car wash and proximity to cell phone towers

You may use Facebook or Twitter to connect with us. If you have questions you may post them.

Can we book an appointment through the web/phone/cell?

You may use the Phone: (416) 223-4253 ext. 26 or Cell: (647) 708-8594 to make appointments

Due to poor signals underground p4 of the building Cell: (647) 708-8594 is not recommended during working hours

Currently we don't have access to the internet underground p4 of the building. Therefore we are unable to allow booking appointments through the web

Where are we located? Where is the entrance and how do we enter?

Location: 5255 Yonge Street Level P4 Toronto, ON M2N 6P4

We are located at Yonge Street and Norton Avenue. The car wash is under the CIBC Building located at Yonge street and Norton Avenue

The entrance is in the Norton Avenue. Once you reach the gate, stop your vehicle and the gate should open automatically. If you have any problems, call (416) 223-4253 ext. 26

There is no mention of our car wash outside the building. You need to go all the way down to parking level 4 (P4) of the building to get to the car wash.
